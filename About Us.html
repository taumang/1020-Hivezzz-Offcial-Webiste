1020 Hivezzz is a honey operation. The number 1020 represents the zip code for Bronkhorstspruit , which is where the hives are located. There are other hives of 1020 which are located in other parts of South Africa. 1020 Hivezzz purpose is to make people aware of the health benefits found in honey and how they integrate it into their daily lives, which enables people to become healthier and stronger.
